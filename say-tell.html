Say vs. Tell

Precision in Biomedical Technical Communication

Verb: Tell

Focuses on the recipient. Mandatory when providing direct instructions or information to a person.

tell + [person] + [information]

Verb: Say

Focuses on the message content. Used when the emphasis is on the actual data or statement.

say + [info] (+ to [person])
